# WooCommerce Setup Guide

## Introduction
This guide will help you configure WooCommerce for setting up an online store on your WordPress site. WooCommerce is a powerful plugin that allows you to manage products, orders, and customers efficiently.

## Step 1: Install WooCommerce
1. Log in to your WordPress Dashboard.
2. Navigate to **Plugins > Add New**.
3. Search for **WooCommerce** and click **Install Now**.
4. After installation, click **Activate**.

## Step 2: WooCommerce Setup Wizard
1. Once activated, a setup wizard will guide you through the initial configuration.
2. Follow the prompts to set your store location, currency, and product types.
3. Set up payment methods and shipping options.

## Step 3: Add Products
1. Navigate to **Products > Add New**.
2. Fill in the product title, description, and set the product data (simple, grouped, variable).
3. Set images and product categories.
4. Publish the product.

## Step 4: Configure Settings
1. Go to **WooCommerce > Settings**. Here you can configure:
   - General settings (location, currency, etc.)
   - Products settings (measurements, inventory)
   - Shipping settings (zones, methods)
   - Payments settings (choose payment gateways)

## Step 5: Set Up Pages
Make sure WooCommerce has created the necessary pages:
- Shop
- Cart
- Checkout
- My Account

If not, you can create new pages and assign them from the WooCommerce settings.

## Step 6: Customize Your Store
1. Use a WooCommerce-compatible theme to enhance the look and feel.
2. Customize your theme settings from **Appearance > Customize**.

## Step 7: Test Your Store
- Place a test order to ensure everything works correctly.
- Check for email notifications and order details.

## Conclusion
Congratulations! Your WooCommerce store is now set up and ready for business. Regularly check for updates and backups to keep everything running smoothly.
